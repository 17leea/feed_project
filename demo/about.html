About feed

feed was created as a final project for a Girls Who Code Immersion Program. Created by Alicia, Egypt, Kelli, Isabela, and Yingquin in August of 2016 at Akamai in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, feed is a website that showcases a lot of the skills each girl learned throughout the 7-week program.

"With the importance of visual appeal at the forefront of our minds, we wanted to create an easy way for people to find food they will love." -Isabela
